---
title: Chicken Shawarma
category: [Dinner, Chicken]
description: Chicken pieces tossed in warm Middle Eastern spices, then baked until tender and served in pita with salad and sauce — no rotisserie needed.
image: images/chicken-shawarma.jpg
size: 6 servings
time: 40 minutes, plus marinating
source: https://www.themediterraneandish.com/chicken-shawarma-recipe/
---

* `¾ tbsp` ground cumin
* `¾ tbsp` turmeric powder
* `¾ tbsp` ground coriander
* `¾ tbsp` garlic powder
* `¾ tbsp` paprika
* `½ tsp` ground cloves
* `½ tsp` cayenne pepper, more if you prefer
* salt
* `8` boneless, skinless chicken thighs
* `1` large onion, thinly sliced
* `1` large lemon, juice of
* `⅓ cup` extra virgin olive oil
* `6` pita pockets
* [tahini sauce](tahini-sauce.html) or Greek [tzatziki sauce](tzatziki-sauce.html)
* baby arugula
* [Mediterranean salad](mediterranean-salad.html)
* pickles or kalamata olives (optional)

> In a small bowl, mix the cumin, turmeric, coriander, garlic powder, paprika, cloves, and cayenne. Set aside the shawarma spice mix for now.

---

> Pat the chicken thighs dry and season with salt on both sides, then thinly slice into small bite-sized pieces.

---

> Place the chicken in a large bowl. Add the shawarma spices and toss to coat. Add the onions, lemon juice, and olive oil. Toss everything together again. Cover and refrigerate for 3 hours or overnight (if you don't have time, you can cut short or skip the marinating time).

---

> When ready, preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Take the chicken out of the fridge and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes.

---

> Spread the marinated chicken and onions in one layer on a large lightly-oiled baking sheet. Roast for 30 minutes. For a more browned, crispier chicken, move the pan to the top rack and broil very briefly (watch carefully). Remove from the oven.

---

> While the chicken roasts, prepare the pita pockets, [tahini sauce](tahini-sauce.html) or [tzatziki sauce](tzatziki-sauce.html), and a simple [Mediterranean salad](mediterranean-salad.html). Set aside.

---

> To serve, open the pita pockets up. Spread a little tahini or tzatziki sauce, add the chicken shawarma, arugula, Mediterranean salad, and pickles or olives, if you like. Serve immediately.
