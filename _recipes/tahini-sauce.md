---
title: Tahini Sauce
category: Sides
description: A creamy, tangy tahini sauce with a fresh parsley twist — great on sandwiches, wraps, bowls, falafel, and mezze platters.
image: images/tahini-sauce.jpg
size: 12 tbsp (serves 5)
time: 10 minutes
source: https://www.themediterraneandish.com/tahini-sauce-recipe-how-to-make-tahini/
---

* `1-2` garlic cloves, peeled
* `½ tsp` salt
* `¾ cup` tahini paste
* `½ cup` fresh lime or lemon juice
* `¼ cup` cold water, about
* `1 cup` chopped parsley (optional)

> Make a garlic paste. Using a mortar and pestle, crush the garlic cloves with the salt into a paste (or mince or finely grate the garlic and season with salt).

---

> Add the crushed garlic, tahini paste, and lime juice to the bowl of a food processor and blend (it will be thick as it emulsifies). Add the water, a little bit at a time, until you reach your desired consistency.

---

> Transfer to a serving bowl and stir in the parsley, if using. Enjoy!
